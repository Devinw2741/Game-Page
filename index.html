The Games

Rock, Paper, Scissors

Rock Paper scissors is a game that is fun for everyone! The rules ar that the opponent is unlikely to play rock again, scissors would be unbeatable. In case of paper, scissors wins; should the opponent choose scissors, it's a tie. RPS players mentally categorize their throws as winners and losers. A player who loses is more likely to switch to a different throw the next time
